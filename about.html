Devsattt Logistics Ltd

Devsattt Logistics is a distinguished clearing and forwarding company based in Nigeria, officially registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of 1990. Our corporate head office is strategically located in No 28 Adeyemo Street, 7&8 Bustop, Isolo, Lagos State, Nigeria. Over the past decade, Devsattt has established itself as a leading provider of clearing and forwarding services, logistics consultancy, freight handling, haulage services, warehousing, and general maritime contracts, evolving into an internationally recognized brand.

In the highly competitive and trust-sensitive logistics industry, Devsattt Logistics Ltd. has earned a stellar reputation for its unwavering commitment to efficiency, reliability, and innovation. Our success stems from a deep-seated dedication to fostering trust and delivering superior service quality. We leverage our extensive industry knowledge and strategic connections to ensure the seamless execution of all logistics operations.

At Devsattt Logistics Ltd., we prioritize offering our clients a competitive edge through our tailored operational approaches. We pride ourselves on delivering personalized attention to each customer, crafting bespoke solutions that address their unique needs within the maritime industry. From providing expert import advice to ensuring timely cargo delivery, we are dedicated to offering the highest standards of client-centric clearing and forwarding services in Nigeria. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction is deeply embedded in our organizational culture. We continuously strive to enhance our service offerings, driven by a passion for excellence and a genuine desire to support our clients' success.
