Devsattt Logistics Ltd

Devsattt Logistics is a distinguished clearing and forwarding company based in Nigeria, officially registered under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of 1990. Our corporate head office is strategically located in No 28 Adeyemo Street, 7&8 Bustop, Isolo, Lagos State, Nigeria. Over the past decade, Devsattt has established itself as a leading provider of clearing and forwarding services, logistics consultancy, freight handling, haulage services, warehousing, and general maritime contracts, evolving into an internationally recognized brand.
